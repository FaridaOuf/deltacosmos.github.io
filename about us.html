Team Delta Cosmos deals with the primary objective of "Space Selfie" which is basically taking pictures from space. We plan to obtain this goal by launching a 3U cubesat to the Low Earth Orbit region(Polar Orbit) to access every part of the world. The satellite is going to help in montoring live images of any location requested by the users. The satellite can also play a part of Natural Disaster and Geospatial Monitoring while simultaneously being the eyes of the public users from space.

SatelliteSnaps, developed by Delta Cosmos—a team of four members from India, Saudi Arabia, and Egypt—is a mobile application designed to make space exploration and astrophotography accessible to the general public. The app aims to provide users with the experience of traveling to space and enjoying spectacular views by allowing them to take their own pictures from space using a satellite. To make this experience accessible, SatelliteSnaps eliminates the need for specialized equipment or software downloads. Users can plan and capture pictures using the satellite, learn how to digitally process images, and enjoy color processing within the app. The app also features a minigame where users can try to find objects like planets and the moon at night, competing to capture a picture the fastest. Additionally, SatelliteSnaps provides information about major astronomical events, daily quizzes, and opportunities to learn more about astronomy. By connecting users with the beauty of space and fostering a deeper appreciation for our fragile planet, SatelliteSnaps aims to make astrophotography and space exploration more accessible and engaging for everyone. Hopefully this app will make people realize how fragile our home planet is .
